Onmyoji

About Onmyoji

Onmyoji is a turn-based strategy game between two players, each player brings 5 ​​different shikigami to fight including Onmyoji, whoever destroys the entire enemy team will be the winner.

Interesting Features of Onmyoji

  • Various skills of each shikigami
  • EEye catching
  • Rich gameplay (PVP, PVE)
  • Many interesting events for players
